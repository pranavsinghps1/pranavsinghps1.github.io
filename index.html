Pranav Singh

Research Scientist · NYU Langone Health

Google Scholar Email LinkedIn

I am a Research Scientist at NYU Langone Health, working with Dr. Miriam Bredella, Prof. Krzysztof Geras, and Prof. Artie Shen on survival analysis, opportunistic imaging, and representation learning for medical imaging. Previously, I worked with Prof. Sumit Chopra and Prof. Geras on end-to-end pipelines for lesion detection and classification on multi-modal mammography scans.

Prior to this, I was an Assistant Research Scientist at the NYU Center for Data Science, developing deep learning methods for medical image analysis under Prof. Jacopo Cirrone. I hold a Master's in Computer Science from NYU (2023), with research on dense prediction and classification for medical imaging — resulting in three publications at ECCV 2022, ICCV 2023, and MLHC 2023.

My undergraduate work at Manipal University Jaipur focused on autonomous rover systems for the University Rover Challenge 2020, where our team reached the international semi-finals. I have contributed to Facebook AI's VISSL and Lightly AI's SSL open-source projects, implementing DeepMind's BYOL approach. I also co-founded the Hult Prize chapter at Manipal, tripling the campus incubation rate.
